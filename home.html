Welcome to FAST University Islamabad

FAST National University of Computer and Emerging Sciences (FAST-NUCES) is one of Pakistan's leading institutions of higher education, known for its excellence in teaching, research, and innovation. Established in 2000, the university has quickly gained a reputation for its rigorous academic programs, cutting-edge research, and strong emphasis on entrepreneurship and industry collaboration.

The Islamabad campus, situated in a serene environment with modern facilities, offers a wide range of undergraduate, graduate, and doctoral programs in disciplines such as Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Business Administration, and Social Sciences. The university is committed to nurturing talent, fostering critical thinking, and preparing students to meet the challenges of the ever-evolving global landscape.

With a strong focus on both academic and extracurricular activities, FAST-NUCES encourages students to engage in research projects, participate in national and international competitions, and develop leadership skills through various societies and clubs. The university's alumni network is a testament to its impact, with graduates excelling in top-tier organizations and contributing to the development of the nation.

At FAST-NUCES, we believe in empowering our students to become innovative professionals and responsible global citizens. Join us in our journey towards academic excellence and making a difference in the world.
